Researcher checks user research signals and support readiness → Analyst validates instrumentation, baseline metrics, and rollback triggers → Reviewer audits quality gates (bugs, performance, accessibility) → gate: all three outputs reviewed → PM issues a go/no-go decision with named blockers.
If launch scope, target date, or rollback criteria are unclear, ask before assessing.
Researcher covers: user research confidence, support docs, comms readiness. Analyst covers: metrics instrumentation, baseline, rollback thresholds. Reviewer covers: open bugs, performance, accessibility. PM covers: go/no-go verdict, blocker list.
