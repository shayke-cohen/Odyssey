---
name: "Quarterly priorities"
sortOrder: 2
---

Researcher surfaces user needs, retention signals, and support themes → Analyst scores opportunities by impact, confidence, and effort → gate: scored opportunity list agreed → PM ranks priorities and writes a one-paragraph justification per item.
If the team's strategic bets or OKRs for the quarter are missing, ask before researching.
Researcher covers: user pain signals, churn drivers, usage patterns. Analyst covers: impact sizing, effort estimate, confidence rating. PM covers: priority ranking, strategic alignment, trade-off rationale.
