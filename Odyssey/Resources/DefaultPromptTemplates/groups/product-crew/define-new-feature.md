Researcher maps the competitive landscape and surfaces user signals → gate: research summary agreed before framing → PM frames the problem and drafts a PRD (problem, user stories, success metrics, non-goals) → Analyst validates the metrics and sizing assumptions.
If the target user segment or business goal are unclear, ask before researching.
Researcher covers: competitor features, user pain signals, market gaps. PM covers: problem framing, scope, success metrics. Analyst covers: metric validity, sizing, instrumentation plan.
