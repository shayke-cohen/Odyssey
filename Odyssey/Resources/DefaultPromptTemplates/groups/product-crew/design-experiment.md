PM frames the hypothesis (if we do X, metric Y will change by Z) → gate: hypothesis agreed before design → Analyst specs the experiment (sample size, assignment, primary/guardrail metrics, duration) → Researcher plans the qualitative follow-up to explain the quantitative result.
If the product question lacks a measurable outcome or affected surface, ask before framing.
PM covers: hypothesis, decision criteria, minimum detectable effect. Analyst covers: statistical design, metric selection, instrumentation. Researcher covers: qualitative method, interview guide, synthesis plan.
