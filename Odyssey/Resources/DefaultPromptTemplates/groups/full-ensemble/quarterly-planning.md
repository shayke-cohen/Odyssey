If the current quarter, OKRs, or team size are unknown, ask before starting.
PM proposes the top 3 bets with rationale; Engineering sizes each bet and flags dependencies; output: `q-proposals.md`.
Design and Growth each review `q-proposals.md` and add feasibility or opportunity notes.
PM consolidates input into a final `q-plan.md` with owner, milestone dates, and cut criteria for each bet.
Gate: all roles must confirm the plan before it is treated as final.
