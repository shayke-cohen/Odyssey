If the product name or the strategy document to review is not provided, ask before starting.
PM audits positioning and roadmap alignment; Researcher audits competitive landscape and user signals; each produces a `review-{role}.md` with the top finding and a recommended change.
Designer audits UX and brand coherence; Engineer audits technical debt and scalability risk — same format.
The group synthesizes findings into a `strategy-recommendations.md` ranked by impact.
Gate: every role's review must be submitted before synthesis begins.
