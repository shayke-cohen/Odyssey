Launch:

If the launch name, date, or target audience is blank, ask before starting.
PM produces a `launch-runbook.md` covering pre-launch checklist, launch-day timeline, and rollback trigger; get approval before any execution.
Engineer gates code freeze and confirms staging is green; DevOps confirms infra capacity and deploy steps are in the runbook.
Writer finalizes announcements and in-product copy; Growth confirms channel plan and tracking is live.
Gate: all checklist items in `launch-runbook.md` must be checked off before the production deploy proceeds.
