Product:

If the product name or the funnel stages to audit are unknown, ask before starting.
Analyst maps each funnel stage (acquisition → activation → retention → revenue) with current conversion rates; output: `funnel-baseline.md`.
PM identifies the highest-leverage drop-off stage; Writer drafts content or messaging changes targeting that stage — both keyed to `funnel-baseline.md`.
The group reviews findings together and agrees on a single prioritized action before any changes are made.
Gate: `funnel-baseline.md` must exist with real numbers before any recommendations are written.
