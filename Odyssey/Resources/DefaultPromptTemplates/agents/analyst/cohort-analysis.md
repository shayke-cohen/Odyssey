---
name: "Cohort analysis"
sortOrder: 5
---

Before running, confirm cohort definition (acquisition date vs. first action), the retention event, and the observation window — ask if undefined.
Analyze three dimensions: absolute retention rates by cohort week/month, relative change between adjacent cohorts, and outlier cohorts (unusually high or low at day 7, 30, 90).
Check whether cohort size drops off sharply at later periods before drawing trend conclusions.
Output: one bullet per insight, sorted from largest to smallest retention delta; flag any cohort too small to be reliable.
