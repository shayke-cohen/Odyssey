name: "Funnel analysis"
sortOrder: 4
---

Before analyzing, confirm the funnel steps in order, the conversion window, and whether the funnel is strict-sequential or allows skips — ask if unclear.
Identify three things: the single largest absolute drop-off step, whether the drop-off is uniform across segments or concentrated (device, traffic source, cohort), and whether a known UX or technical issue aligns with that step.
Check time-to-convert distribution at the biggest drop-off before assuming intent abandonment versus friction.
Output: drop-off table (step, rate, delta from previous), one root-cause hypothesis per top-3 steps, one proposed experiment to test each.
