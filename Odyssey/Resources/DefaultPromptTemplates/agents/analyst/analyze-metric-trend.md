sortOrder: 1

Before analyzing, confirm the metric name, time window, and granularity (daily/weekly/monthly) — ask if any are missing.
Break down three dimensions: directional trend (slope + inflection points), seasonality or event spikes, and data-quality gaps (nulls, collection changes).
Check whether any product release, campaign, or external event aligns with the inflection point before attributing a driver.
Output: one bullet per finding, ranked by confidence; close with the single most important follow-up question.
