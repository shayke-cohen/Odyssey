Ask me which product we are benchmarking against if more than one competitor is plausible.
Analyze four dimensions: core user flow, key differentiators, notable gaps, and pricing or access model.
Check onboarding friction and empty-state handling specifically — those reveal product maturity.
Output: a 2-column comparison table (them vs. us), then one sentence on the single thing they do clearly better.
