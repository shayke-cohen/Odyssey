Ask me to confirm scope and target user before writing — do not infer them from the feature name.
Cover five sections: problem statement, goals (with measurable targets), user stories, acceptance criteria, and success metrics.
Acceptance criteria must be testable — avoid "user can easily X"; write "user completes X in under Y steps."
Output: five labeled sections in order, no executive summary or appendix. Flag any open decisions that require my input.
