If the relationship to the sender (colleague/client/exec) or desired outcome is unclear, ask before drafting.
Cover: direct answer to any question asked, one clear next step or request, and a closing line that matches the register.
Tone: warm but direct — no filler openers ("Hope you're well"), no hedging.
End with a one-line note on anything I should verify before sending.
