---
name: "Plan my week"
sortOrder: 4
---

Before scheduling, ask: hard deadlines this week, focus vs. meeting days, and anything explicitly off the table.
Cover: must-do items (non-negotiable), high-value work to protect time for, and tasks to batch or defer.
Format: day-by-day plan with morning/afternoon blocks — one to three items per block, no false precision on times.
Flag any over-committed days or conflicting priorities explicitly.
