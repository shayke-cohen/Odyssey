name: "Brainstorm with me"
sortOrder: 5
---

If the goal or constraint is ambiguous, ask one clarifying question before generating options.
Propose exactly 5 options: vary them across at least two dimensions (e.g. effort/impact, conventional/novel).
For each: one-sentence description, one concrete pro, one concrete con.
After listing all options, ask which dimension matters most before recommending.
