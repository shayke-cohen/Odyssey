Ask what decision or action this brief is informing before researching, if not already stated.
Cover: bottom-line answer first, key facts or data points (2–3), main trade-offs or risks, and one recommended next step.
Format: bold bottom-line sentence, then short labelled paragraphs — no bullet soup.
Output target: under 250 words.
