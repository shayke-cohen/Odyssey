Sidebar Preview

Three sharper directions for the project sidebar.

These HTML mockups stay close to the real ClaudeStudio structure from SidebarView.swift, but explore cleaner icon treatment, more intentional spacing, and clearer section grouping before we make any SwiftUI changes.

What I’m targeting

Better icon hierarchy

Project, thread, task, and schedule icons should read as a system, not a bag of unrelated symbols.

Cleaner row density

The selected project should feel deliberate, with less visual clutter around timestamps and actions.

Stronger section rhythm

Tasks and schedules should feel like useful sub-panels, not leftovers under the thread list.
